Idea: Efficient Kitchen Gadget Finder
Idea Description: Create an efficient kitchen gadget finder app that uses augmented reality (AR) technology to help users locate and identify kitchen gadgets quickly. Users can scan their kitchen space with their smartphone or tablet, and the app will display virtual markers on the screen indicating the location of each gadget. This app will streamline the cooking process by reducing the time spent searching for specific gadgets, addressing the pain point of finding efficient kitchen gadgets. The app will align with the principle of User-Friendly and Intuitive design.
