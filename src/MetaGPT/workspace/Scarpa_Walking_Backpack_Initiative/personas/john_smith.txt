
Journey Map: 
John wakes up early in the morning and prepares for his day. He packs his walking backpack with essentials like water, snacks, and a map. He leaves his apartment and starts his walk in the nearby park, enjoying the fresh air and tranquility. After a few hours, he stops for a break, unpacks his backpack, and enjoys a snack. He then continues his walk, exploring different trails. By late afternoon, he returns home, feeling refreshed and satisfied with his day.
