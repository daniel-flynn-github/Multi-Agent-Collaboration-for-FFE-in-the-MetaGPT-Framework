Idea: Modular Scalability Architecture
Idea Description: Implement a modular scalability architecture for the robotic platform, allowing users to easily scale up their robotic fleet by adding more robots that can work together seamlessly. Each robot would be equipped with communication modules that enable them to coordinate tasks and share data, creating a networked system that can efficiently handle complex operations. By designing the platform with scalability in mind, users can expand their robotic workforce without facing integration or performance issues. This idea addresses the pain point of scalability issues due to customization limitations by providing a scalable and interconnected solution.
