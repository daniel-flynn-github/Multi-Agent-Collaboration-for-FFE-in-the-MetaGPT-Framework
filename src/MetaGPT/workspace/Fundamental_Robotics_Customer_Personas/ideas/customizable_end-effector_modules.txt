Idea: Customizable End-Effector Modules
Idea Description: Develop a range of interchangeable end-effector modules for the robotic arm that can be easily swapped out to adapt the robot for specific healthcare applications. These modules could include grippers, suction cups, force sensors, and specialized tools for medical tasks. By providing a variety of options, users can customize the robot for different tasks without the need for extensive reprogramming or hardware modifications. This addresses the pain point of limited customization options for healthcare applications by offering a flexible and user-friendly solution.
