Idea: Climbing Accessory Rental Stations
Idea Description: Scarpa can partner with climbing gyms and outdoor retailers to set up climbing accessory rental stations. These stations would be equipped with a range of climbing accessories, including harnesses, carabiners, helmets, and other essential gear. Climbers can rent the required accessories for their outdoor climbing trips, eliminating the need for them to purchase expensive gear upfront. This rental service would provide a cost-effective solution for climbers transitioning outdoors while also promoting the use of high-quality gear.
