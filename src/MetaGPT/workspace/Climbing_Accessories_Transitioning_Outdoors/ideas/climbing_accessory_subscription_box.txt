Idea: Climbing Accessory Subscription Box
Idea Description: Scarpa can introduce a climbing accessory subscription box service targeted towards intermediate climbers transitioning to outdoor climbing. Subscribers would receive a curated box of climbing accessories on a monthly or quarterly basis. The box could include items such as chalk, tape, carabiners, and other essential gear.
