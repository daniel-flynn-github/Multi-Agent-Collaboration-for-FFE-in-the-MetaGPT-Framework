Idea: Outdoor Climbing Mentorship Program
Idea Description: Establish an outdoor climbing mentorship program that pairs experienced outdoor climbers with intermediate climbers transitioning from indoor to outdoor climbing. The program would provide mentorship, guidance, and support to help climbers navigate the challenges and nuances of outdoor climbing. Mentors would share their expertise, offer advice on gear selection, route planning, and safety considerations, and accompany mentees on climbing trips to provide hands-on guidance. This mentorship program would foster a sense of community and facilitate knowledge transfer, allowing climbers to learn from experienced individuals and gain confidence in their outdoor climbing abilities.
