
Journey Map: 
Alex joins a group of volunteers for a trail cleanup in a local nature reserve. He carries eco-friendly supplies like reusable gloves, trash bags, and water bottles in his environmentally responsible walking backpack. Walking through the forest, Alex collects litter and educates others about the importance of preserving natural habitats. His backpack reflects his commitment to sustainability and serves as a conversation starter with fellow participants. After a successful cleanup, Alex relaxes by a tranquil stream, appreciating the beauty of the restored trail and the convenience of his eco-conscious backpack for outdoor activities.
