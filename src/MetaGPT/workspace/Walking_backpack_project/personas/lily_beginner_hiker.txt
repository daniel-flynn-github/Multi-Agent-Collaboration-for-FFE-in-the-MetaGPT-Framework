
Journey Map: 
Lily sets out on her first solo hike in a nearby national park. She packs her user-friendly walking backpack with essentials like a trail map, sunscreen, a light jacket, and a snack. Walking along the marked trail, Lily encounters gentle slopes and scenic viewpoints, feeling a sense of wonder and excitement in nature. Her backpack's simple design and adjustable straps make it easy to carry and access her belongings on the trail. As she reaches a lookout point, Lily takes a break to admire the expansive landscape and snap photos with her smartphone from the backpack. Returning to the trailhead, Lily feels proud of her hiking accomplishment and looks forward to more outdoor adventures.
