
Journey Map: 
David prepares for a multi-day expedition into the wilderness. He carefully organizes his professional equipment, including a first aid kit, navigation tools, and camping gear, in his durable walking backpack. Setting out on the rugged trail, David encounters challenging terrains and changing weather conditions. His backpack withstands the rough environment and keeps his belongings secure. During the journey, David guides his group through steep slopes and dense forests, relying on his backpack for easy access to essential items. As night falls, David sets up camp and rests, appreciating the reliability and functionality of his backpack in the wilderness.
