Idea: Outdoor Climbing Mentorship Program
Idea Description: Establish an outdoor climbing mentorship program that pairs experienced outdoor climbers with intermediate boulder and sport climbers who are transitioning from indoor climbing. The mentorship program should provide guidance, support, and practical knowledge to help climbers navigate the challenges and nuances of outdoor climbing. Mentors can offer personalized advice, accompany climbers on outdoor climbing trips, and provide hands-on training and feedback. The mentorship program can be facilitated through an online platform that connects mentors and mentees based on their climbing preferences and goals.
