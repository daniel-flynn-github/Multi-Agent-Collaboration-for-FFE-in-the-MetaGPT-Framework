Idea: Outdoor Climbing Guidebook
Idea Description: Create a comprehensive guidebook specifically designed for intermediate boulder and sport climbers who are transitioning from indoor climbing to outdoor climbing. The guidebook should include detailed information about outdoor climbing locations, routes, difficulty levels, safety precautions, and tips for transitioning from indoor to outdoor climbing. It should also provide guidance on equipment selection and usage for outdoor climbing. The guidebook can be in a portable format, such as a waterproof and tear-resistant booklet, making it easy for climbers to carry in their backpacks.
