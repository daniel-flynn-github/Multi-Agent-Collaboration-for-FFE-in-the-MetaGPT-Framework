Idea: Portable Climbing Wall
Idea Description: Design a portable climbing wall that can be easily transported and set up at outdoor climbing locations. The climbing wall should mimic the features and challenges of natural rock formations, allowing climbers to practice their skills and techniques in a controlled environment before attempting them on real rocks. The portable climbing wall should be lightweight, collapsible, and durable, making it convenient for climbers to carry in their backpacks. It can be constructed using modular panels that can be assembled and disassembled quickly.
