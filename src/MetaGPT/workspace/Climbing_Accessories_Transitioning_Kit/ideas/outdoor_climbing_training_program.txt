Idea: Outdoor Climbing Training Program
Idea Description: Develop a specialized training program tailored for intermediate boulder and sport climbers who are transitioning from indoor climbing to outdoor climbing. The program should focus on building the necessary skills and techniques required for outdoor climbing, such as route reading, anchor building, and lead climbing. The training program can be delivered through a combination of online resources, instructional videos, and in-person workshops to ensure accessibility for climbers.
