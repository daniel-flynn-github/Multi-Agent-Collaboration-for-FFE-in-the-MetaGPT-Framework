Idea: Outdoor Climbing Safety Kit
Idea Description: Develop a comprehensive safety kit specifically designed for intermediate boulder and sport climbers transitioning from indoor climbing to outdoor climbing. The safety kit should include essential safety equipment, such as a helmet, harness, carabiners, and quickdraws, along with instructional materials on proper usage and maintenance. Additionally, the safety kit can include a compact first aid kit tailored for outdoor climbing emergencies. The safety kit should be lightweight, compact, and easily packable in a backpack.
