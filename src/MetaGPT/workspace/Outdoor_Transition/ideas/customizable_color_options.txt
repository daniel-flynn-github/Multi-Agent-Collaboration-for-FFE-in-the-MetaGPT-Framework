Idea Description: Introduce a feature that allows customers to customize the color of their climbing accessories. This can be done through an online platform or in-store kiosks where customers can choose from a range of color options for different parts of the accessory, such as straps, buckles, or padding. By offering a wide variety of color choices, customers can personalize their gear and express their individual style, addressing the pain point of limited color options.
