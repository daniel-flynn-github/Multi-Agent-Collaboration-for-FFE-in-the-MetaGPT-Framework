Idea: Interchangeable Color Panels
Idea Description: Design climbing accessories with interchangeable color panels. These panels can be easily swapped out to change the appearance of the gear. Customers can purchase additional color panels separately, allowing them to mix and match colors according to their preferences. This innovative feature provides a cost-effective way for customers to have multiple color options without needing to purchase multiple accessories, addressing the pain point of limited color options.
