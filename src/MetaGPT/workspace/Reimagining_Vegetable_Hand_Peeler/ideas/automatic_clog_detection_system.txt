Idea: Automatic Clog Detection System
Idea Description: Implement an automatic clog detection system in the vegetable hand peeler to address frequent clogging during peeling. Utilize sensors to monitor the peeling process and detect any signs of clogging. When a clog is detected, the system can automatically reverse the peeling direction or adjust the peeling speed to clear the blockage. This intelligent feature will prevent interruptions during peeling, ensuring a smooth and efficient experience for the user.
