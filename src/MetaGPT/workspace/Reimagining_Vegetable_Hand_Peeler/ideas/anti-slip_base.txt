Idea: Anti-Slip Base
Idea Description: Incorporate an anti-slip base in the vegetable hand peeler to prevent handle discomfort after prolonged use. The base of the peeler can feature a non-slip material or suction cups that securely anchor it to the countertop. This stability ensures that the user can apply consistent pressure while peeling without the peeler slipping or sliding. By enhancing stability, the anti-slip base promotes a safer and more comfortable peeling experience for the user.
