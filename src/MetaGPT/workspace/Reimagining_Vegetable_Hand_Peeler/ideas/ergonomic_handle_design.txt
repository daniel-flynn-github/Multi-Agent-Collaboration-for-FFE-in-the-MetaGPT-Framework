Idea: Ergonomic Handle Design
Idea Description: Redesign the handle of the vegetable hand peeler to address handle discomfort after prolonged use. Utilize ergonomic principles to create a handle that fits comfortably in the user's hand, reducing strain and fatigue. The handle can be made of soft, non-slip material to provide a secure grip. Additionally, incorporating adjustable features such as handle angle or size customization can further enhance user comfort and prevent hand cramps during extended peeling sessions.
