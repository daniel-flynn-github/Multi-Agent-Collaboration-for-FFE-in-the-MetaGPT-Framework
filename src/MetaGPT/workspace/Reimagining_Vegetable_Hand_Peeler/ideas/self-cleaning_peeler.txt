Idea: Self-Cleaning Peeler
Idea Description: Introduce a self-cleaning mechanism in the vegetable hand peeler to address the challenge of cleaning the peeler. The peeler can have a detachable compartment where water and a cleaning solution can be added. Upon activation, the peeler rotates within the compartment, ensuring thorough cleaning of the blades and removing any residue. This feature will save time and effort for users, promoting hygiene and preventing clogging issues.
