Idea: Removable Blade Cartridge
Idea Description: Design the vegetable hand peeler with a removable blade cartridge to address challenges with cleaning the peeler. The blade cartridge can be easily detached from the main body for thorough cleaning or replacement. This modular design allows users to maintain hygiene by washing the blades separately and ensures that any stubborn residue can be effectively removed. The removable blade cartridge simplifies maintenance and prolongs the lifespan of the peeler.
