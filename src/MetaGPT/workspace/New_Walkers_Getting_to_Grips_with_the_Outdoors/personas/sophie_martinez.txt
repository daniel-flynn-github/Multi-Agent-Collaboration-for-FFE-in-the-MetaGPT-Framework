
Journey Map: 
During a school break, Sophie plans a solo hiking adventure to challenge herself. She packs her walking backpack with essentials like a compass, energy bars, and a camera. As she explores new trails, she adjusts the backpack for balance and ease of movement. Sophie encounters obstacles like fallen trees and narrow paths, testing her skills. She pauses to sketch the scenery and reflect on her journey. After a fulfilling hike, she returns home with a sense of achievement and tranquility.
