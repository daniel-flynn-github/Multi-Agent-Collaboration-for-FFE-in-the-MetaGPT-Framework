Idea: Photo Storage Solution
Idea Description: Integrate a detachable, lightweight camera holder into the backpack design, allowing users to securely store their cameras for easy access. The holder should have padding to protect the camera and adjustable straps for customization. Additionally, include a dedicated pocket with shock-absorbent material to store memory cards safely. This feature addresses the pain point of limited phone storage for photos by providing a dedicated space for cameras, ensuring that users can capture their outdoor adventures without worrying about running out of phone storage.
