Idea: Interactive Learning Panel
Idea Description: Integrate an interactive learning panel into the walking backpack, featuring a digital display that provides educational content, survival tips, and interactive challenges for users. This panel can be accessed via a smartphone app, allowing walkers to engage with nature in a fun and informative way. By promoting educational experiences during outdoor activities, this idea aligns with the project's goal of inspiring a connection with nature. The learning panel would incorporate a touchscreen display, GPS tracking capabilities, and interactive modules for engaging user experiences.
