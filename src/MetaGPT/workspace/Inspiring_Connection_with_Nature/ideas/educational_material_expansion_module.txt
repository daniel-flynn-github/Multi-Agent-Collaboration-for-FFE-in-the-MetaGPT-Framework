Idea: Educational Material Expansion Module
Idea Description: Develop a detachable expansion module for the walking backpack specifically designed to accommodate educational materials such as maps, guides, and notebooks. This module can be easily attached to the main backpack, providing additional space without compromising the overall weight distribution. By addressing the pain point of inadequate space for educational materials, users can now carry essential learning resources seamlessly during their outdoor adventures. The module would incorporate lightweight materials, secure attachment mechanisms, and quick access features for educational items.
