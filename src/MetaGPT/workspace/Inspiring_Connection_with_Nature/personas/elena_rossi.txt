
Journey Map: 
Elena wakes up early on a Saturday morning, excited for her nature walk. She packs her camera gear and essentials into her walking backpack. As she sets off on the trail, she captures the beauty of the sunrise through her lens. During her hike, Elena encounters a serene lake and takes stunning photographs of the reflections. She stops for a snack break, easily accessing her snacks from the backpack. After a few hours of exploration, Elena returns home, feeling rejuvenated and inspired by the nature she experienced.
