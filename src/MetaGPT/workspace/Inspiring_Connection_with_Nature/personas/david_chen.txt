
Journey Map: 
David plans a weekend hiking trip with his friends. He packs his camping equipment into his durable walking backpack. On the trail, David faces challenging terrains but his backpack holds up well, providing the necessary support. He reaches a scenic viewpoint, sets up camp, and enjoys a meal cooked over a campfire. As night falls, David uses his backpack to store his belongings securely. The next morning, he packs up and heads back, feeling accomplished and connected to nature.
