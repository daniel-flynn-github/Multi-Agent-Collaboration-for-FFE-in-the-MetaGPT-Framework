
Journey Map: 
Emma wakes up early on a Saturday morning, excited for her hiking trip. She packs her lightweight walking backpack with essentials like water, snacks, and a map. Emma sets off on a scenic trail, enjoying the sounds of nature and the fresh air. Along the way, she stops to take photos of interesting plants and wildlife. After a few hours of hiking, Emma finds a peaceful spot to rest and have her packed lunch. She appreciates the comfort and convenience of her backpack. As the day comes to an end, Emma returns home feeling rejuvenated and accomplished.
