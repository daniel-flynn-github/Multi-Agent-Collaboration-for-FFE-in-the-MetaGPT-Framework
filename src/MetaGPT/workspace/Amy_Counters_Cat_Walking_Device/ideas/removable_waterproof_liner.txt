Idea: Removable Waterproof Liner
Idea Description: Integrate a removable waterproof liner into the cat walking device to create a barrier between the cat and any external moisture. The liner should be easy to clean, quick to dry, and securely attachable to the device. Use waterproof materials like nylon or polyester that are resistant to water absorption and odor retention. The liner can be machine washable or wipeable for convenient maintenance. Collaborate with textile experts to source high-quality, pet-safe waterproof fabrics for the liner.
