Idea: Adjustable Size Mechanism
Idea Description: Incorporate an adjustable size mechanism into the cat walking device to cater to cats of different sizes. This mechanism can be a sliding feature that extends or retracts the length of the device, allowing it to accommodate both small and large cats comfortably. The adjustable size feature should be user-friendly, durable, and secure to prevent any accidents during use. Utilize lightweight yet sturdy materials like aluminum or reinforced plastic for the adjustable components to maintain the device's portability and durability.
