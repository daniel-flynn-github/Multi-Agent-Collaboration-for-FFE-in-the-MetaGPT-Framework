Idea: Color Wraps
Idea Description: Develop a series of color wraps specifically designed for the cat walking device. These wraps are made of water-resistant, flexible material that can be easily applied and removed without leaving residue. Cat owners can choose from a variety of colors and patterns to change the appearance of the device quickly. The wraps not only address the limited color options but also provide a layer of protection against water damage. To implement this idea, collaborate with a manufacturer specializing in high-quality, adhesive-backed wraps to ensure a perfect fit and easy application for users.
