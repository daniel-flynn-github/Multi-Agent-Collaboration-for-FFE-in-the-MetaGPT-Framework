Idea: Customizable Color Panels
Idea Description: Introduce interchangeable color panels that allow users to personalize their backpack. Offer a range of vibrant color options that can be easily swapped to match different outfits or preferences. Utilize durable hook-and-loop fasteners to secure the panels in place without compromising the backpack's integrity. Ensure the panels are water-resistant and easy to clean for long-term use. Conduct market research to determine popular color trends for maximum appeal.
