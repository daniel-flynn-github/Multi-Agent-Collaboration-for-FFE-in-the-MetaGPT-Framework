Idea: Multi-functional Climbing Backpack
Idea Description: Design a multi-functional climbing backpack specifically tailored for intermediate climbers transitioning from indoor to outdoor climbing. The backpack should have dedicated compartments for climbing gear such as shoes, harness, and helmet, as well as additional storage space for water, snacks, and personal belongings. It should also feature external attachment points for carrying a rope, quickdraws, and other climbing accessories. The backpack should be lightweight, durable, and comfortable to wear during long approaches to climbing areas.
