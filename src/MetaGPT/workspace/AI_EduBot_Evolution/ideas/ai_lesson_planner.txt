Idea: AI Lesson Planner
Idea Description: The AI Lesson Planner is a feature that helps teachers prepare practical AI sessions. It provides a library of ready-to-use activities and lessons that teachers can select and customize according to their needs. The lessons cover a range of topics, from basic AI concepts to advanced machine learning techniques. The AI Lesson Planner uses machine learning to recommend lessons based on the students' level and progress. This feature would be accessible through a web interface, allowing teachers to plan their lessons from any device. This feature addresses the pain point of 'Difficulty in preparing practical AI sessions'.
