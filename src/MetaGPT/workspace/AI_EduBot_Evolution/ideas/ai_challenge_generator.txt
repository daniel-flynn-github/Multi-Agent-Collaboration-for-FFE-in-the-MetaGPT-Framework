Idea: AI Challenge Generator
Idea Description: The AI Challenge Generator is a feature that generates coding challenges based on the AI concepts students have learned. It uses machine learning to create challenges that are tailored to each student's skill level. The AI Challenge Generator provides a fun and engaging way for students to practice their skills and learn new concepts. This feature would be integrated into the robot's programming interface, allowing students to access new challenges at any time. This feature addresses the pain point of 'Struggles with introducing basic AI concepts'.
