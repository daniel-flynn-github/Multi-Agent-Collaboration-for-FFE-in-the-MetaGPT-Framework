Idea: AI Skill Tracker
Idea Description: The AI Skill Tracker is a feature that tracks students' progress and skills development. It uses machine learning to analyze the students' code and identify the AI concepts they have mastered and those they are struggling with. The AI Skill Tracker provides a visual representation of each student's skill set, making it easy for teachers to assess their progress. This feature would be accessible through a web interface, allowing teachers to monitor their students' progress from any device. This feature addresses the pain point of 'Struggles with reviewing students' work'.
