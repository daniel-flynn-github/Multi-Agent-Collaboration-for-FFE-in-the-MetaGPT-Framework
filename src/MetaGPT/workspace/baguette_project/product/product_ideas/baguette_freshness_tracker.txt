Product: Baguette Freshness Tracker
Product Description: A small device that can be attached to a baguette packaging to monitor its freshness. The device uses sensors to detect the level of moisture and temperature inside the packaging. It sends real-time data to a mobile app, allowing customers to know the freshness of the baguette before purchasing.
