Idea: Interactive AI Learning Activities
Idea Description: Another solution to the pain point of struggles to explain complex AI concepts is to have the robot conduct interactive AI learning activities. The robot can guide the students through hands-on activities that involve programming and controlling the robot to perform tasks. This can be achieved by using the robot's sensors and AI algorithms in combination with a user-friendly programming interface. The activities can be customized by the teachers to align with the curriculum and the students' learning levels.
