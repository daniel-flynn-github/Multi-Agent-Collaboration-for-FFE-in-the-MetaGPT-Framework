Idea: Interactive AI Demonstrations
Idea Description: Another solution to the pain point of difficulty in preparing lesson plans is to have the robot perform interactive AI demonstrations. The robot can demonstrate real-life applications of AI algorithms, such as object recognition or path finding, which can be easily understood by the students. This can be achieved by using the robot's camera and sensors in combination with pre-programmed AI algorithms. The demonstrations can be controlled and customized by the teachers via a wireless link with a computer.
