Product: Climbing Chalk Bag
Product Description: The Climbing Chalk Bag is a must-have accessory for intermediate boulder and sport climbers transitioning from indoor climbing to outdoor climbing. It has a spacious interior to accommodate a sufficient amount of chalk and a drawstring closure to prevent chalk spillage. The bag comes with an adjustable waist belt, allowing climbers to easily access the chalk while climbing.
