Product: Outdoor Climbing Helmet
Product Description: The Outdoor Climbing Helmet is a safety essential for intermediate boulder and sport climbers transitioning from indoor climbing to outdoor climbing. This helmet is designed to provide maximum protection against potential head injuries during climbing activities. It features a lightweight and durable construction with impact-absorbing foam and a hard outer shell. The helmet is adjustable to ensure a secure and comfortable fit for different head sizes. With ventilation holes and moisture-wicking padding, it offers breathability and comfort during long climbing sessions. The helmet is compact and can be easily carried in a backpack.
