Product: StickyToe Climbing Shoes
Product Description: The StickyToe Climbing Shoes are designed to provide maximum grip on slippery surfaces. These shoes feature a specially formulated rubber sole that offers superior traction and stickiness. The sole has a unique pattern of small, closely spaced lugs that increase the contact area with the surface, enhancing grip. The shoes also have a toe cap made of a high-friction rubber compound that provides extra grip when toe-hooking on holds. The upper is made of a durable and breathable synthetic material that ensures comfort and support during climbing. The StickyToe Climbing Shoes are lightweight and compact, allowing climbers to easily carry them in a backpack for outdoor climbing.
