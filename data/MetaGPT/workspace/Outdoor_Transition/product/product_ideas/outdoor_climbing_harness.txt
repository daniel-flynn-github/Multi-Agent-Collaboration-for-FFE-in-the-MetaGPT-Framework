Product: Outdoor Climbing Harness
Product Description: Scarpa can introduce an outdoor climbing harness tailored for intermediate climbers making the transition from indoor gyms to outdoor climbing. This harness will prioritize comfort and safety, featuring adjustable leg loops and waist belt for a personalized fit. It will be constructed with durable and high-quality materials to withstand the rigors of outdoor climbing. The harness will also incorporate gear loops and attachment points for carrying essential climbing equipment. To ensure portability, the harness will be lightweight and foldable, easily fitting into a backpack for transport to the crag.
