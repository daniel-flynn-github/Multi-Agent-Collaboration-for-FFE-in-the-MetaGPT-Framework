Product: Climbing Shoe Fitting App
Product Description: This product is a mobile app that helps intermediate boulder and sport climbers find the perfect fit for their outdoor climbing shoes. The app utilizes advanced 3D scanning technology to accurately measure the climber's feet and recommend the most suitable shoe size and model. It also takes into account the climber's preferences, such as shoe stiffness, sensitivity, and closure system. The app provides a virtual try-on feature, allowing climbers to visualize how the shoes will fit and look on their feet. It also offers a database of user reviews and ratings for different climbing shoe models, helping climbers make informed decisions. The app is lightweight and can be easily installed on smartphones, making it accessible for climbers to use on the go.
