Product Description: The Outdoor Climbing Starter Kit is a comprehensive package designed to help intermediate boulder and sport climbers transition from indoor gyms to outdoor climbing. With this kit, climbers can confidently explore outdoor climbing routes while adhering to safety laws and guidelines.
