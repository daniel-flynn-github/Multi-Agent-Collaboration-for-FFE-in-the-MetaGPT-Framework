Product: Outdoor Climbing Guidebook
Product Description: Scarpa can collaborate with experienced outdoor climbers to create a comprehensive climbing guidebook specifically tailored for intermediate climbers transitioning from indoor gyms to outdoor climbing. This guidebook will provide detailed information about popular climbing areas, routes, difficulty levels, and safety considerations. It will include clear and concise route descriptions, topographic maps, and relevant photographs to assist climbers in planning their outdoor climbing adventures. The guidebook will be compact and lightweight, allowing climbers to easily carry it in their backpacks for on-site reference.
