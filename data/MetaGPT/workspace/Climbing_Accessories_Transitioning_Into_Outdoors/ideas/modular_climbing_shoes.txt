Idea: Modular Climbing Shoes
Idea Description: Create modular climbing shoes that offer interchangeable ankle support options. The shoes will have a detachable ankle cuff that can be easily swapped depending on the climber's preference and the type of climbing they are doing. The ankle cuffs will come in different heights and stiffness levels, allowing climbers to customize their level of ankle support. This will provide the flexibility to transition from indoor to outdoor climbing while maintaining the desired ankle stability.
