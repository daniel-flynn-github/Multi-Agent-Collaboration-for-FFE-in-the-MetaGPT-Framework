Idea: Smart Insole with Ankle Support
Idea Description: Design a smart insole with built-in ankle support for climbing shoes. The insole will be equipped with sensors that detect the position and movement of the foot and ankle. Using this data, the insole will provide real-time feedback and support to the ankle joint, helping climbers maintain proper alignment and reducing the risk of injuries. The smart insole will connect to a mobile app, allowing climbers to track their ankle stability and receive personalized recommendations for improvement.
