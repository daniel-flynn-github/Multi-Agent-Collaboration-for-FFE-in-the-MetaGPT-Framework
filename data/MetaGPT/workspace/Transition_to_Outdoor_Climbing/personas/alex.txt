
Journey Map: 
Alex starts his journey by researching about the environmental impact of climbing. He then starts looking for eco-friendly climbing gear. He compares different brands and reads reviews. He is drawn to brands that are sustainable and have a good reputation. He purchases the gear and starts practicing outdoor climbing in a local area. He is mindful of his impact on the environment and follows leave-no-trace principles. He gradually gains experience and starts planning for more challenging climbs.
