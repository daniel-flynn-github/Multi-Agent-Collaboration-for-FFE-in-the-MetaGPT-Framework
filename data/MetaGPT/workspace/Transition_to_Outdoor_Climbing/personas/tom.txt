
Journey Map: 
Tom starts his journey by researching online about the transition from indoor to outdoor climbing. He reads blogs, watches videos, and participates in online climbing communities. He then begins to look for gear, comparing different brands and reading reviews. He is drawn to high-tech gear that promises enhanced performance. He purchases the gear and starts practicing outdoor climbing in a local area under the supervision of an experienced climber. He gradually gains confidence and starts planning for more challenging climbs.
