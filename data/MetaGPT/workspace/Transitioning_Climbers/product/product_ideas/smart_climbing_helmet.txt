Product: Smart Climbing Helmet
Product Description: The Smart Climbing Helmet is a technologically advanced helmet designed specifically for intermediate boulder and sport climbers transitioning to outdoor climbing. This helmet incorporates built-in sensors and a heads-up display (HUD) to provide climbers with real-time route information and navigation assistance. The sensors detect the climber's head movement and orientation, allowing the HUD to display route directions, upcoming obstacles, and key features of the outdoor route. The Smart Climbing Helmet also includes a communication system, enabling climbers to connect with other climbers in the area and receive guidance or assistance. With the Smart Climbing Helmet, climbers can navigate unfamiliar outdoor routes with confidence and enhance their safety during the transition process.
