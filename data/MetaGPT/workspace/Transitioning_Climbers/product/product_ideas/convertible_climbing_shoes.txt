Product: Convertible Climbing Shoes
Product Description: These climbing shoes are designed for intermediate climbers who want to transition from indoor climbing to outdoor climbing. They feature a unique design that allows them to be converted from a comfortable approach shoe to a high-performance climbing shoe. The shoes have a sticky rubber outsole for excellent grip on rock surfaces and a reinforced toe cap for added durability. The lacing system is adjustable to provide a secure fit and support during climbing. The shoes also have a breathable upper to keep the feet cool and dry during intense climbing sessions.
