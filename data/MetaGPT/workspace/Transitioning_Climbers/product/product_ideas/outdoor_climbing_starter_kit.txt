Product: Outdoor Climbing Starter Kit
Product Description: The Outdoor Climbing Starter Kit is a comprehensive package that includes all the essential gear needed for intermediate boulder and sport climbers to transition from indoor climbing gyms to outdoor climbing. The kit includes a harness, climbing shoes, a helmet, a belay device, carabiners, quickdraws, and a chalk bag. All the gear is carefully selected to meet safety standards and provide optimal performance. The kit is designed to be lightweight and compact, allowing climbers to easily transport it in their backpacks to the crag. By offering a complete set of gear at an affordable price, the Outdoor Climbing Starter Kit addresses the pain point of finding affordable climbing gear for transitioning climbers.
