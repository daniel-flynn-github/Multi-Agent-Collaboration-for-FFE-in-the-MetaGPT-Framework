Product: Outdoor Climbing Skills Workshop
Product Description: The Outdoor Climbing Skills Workshop is a hands-on training program designed specifically for intermediate boulder and sport climbers who want to transition into outdoor climbing. Participants will have the opportunity to practice these skills in real outdoor climbing environments, under the guidance and supervision of the instructors. The workshop aims to provide transitioning climbers with the knowledge, confidence, and practical experience necessary to safely and successfully climb outdoors.
