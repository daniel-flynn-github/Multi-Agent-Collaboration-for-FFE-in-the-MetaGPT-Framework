Idea: Online Transitioning Climbing Community
Idea Description: Create an online platform dedicated to supporting climbers transitioning from indoor to outdoor climbing. The platform will provide a space for climbers to connect, share experiences, seek advice, and find climbing partners. It will feature forums, discussion boards, instructional videos, and a database of outdoor climbing locations. The online community will foster a sense of belonging and provide a supportive network for climbers, addressing the lack of guidance and support by connecting climbers from around the world.
