Idea: Transitioning Climbing Gear Rental Service
Idea Description: Introduce a transitioning climbing gear rental service that offers a curated selection of gear specifically designed for climbers transitioning from indoor to outdoor climbing. The rental service will include essential gear such as climbing shoes, harnesses, helmets, and belay devices, ensuring climbers have access to the appropriate equipment without the need for a significant upfront investment. The gear rental service will address the pain point of affordability and accessibility, allowing climbers to try out different gear options before making a purchase.
