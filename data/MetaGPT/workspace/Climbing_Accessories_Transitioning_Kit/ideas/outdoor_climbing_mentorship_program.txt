Idea: Outdoor Climbing Mentorship Program
Idea Description: Establish an outdoor climbing mentorship program where experienced outdoor climbers are paired with intermediate climbers transitioning to outdoor climbing. The mentorship program will provide one-on-one guidance, support, and advice to climbers, helping them navigate the challenges of outdoor climbing. This mentorship program will address the lack of guidance by connecting climbers with experienced mentors who can offer personalized support and encouragement.
