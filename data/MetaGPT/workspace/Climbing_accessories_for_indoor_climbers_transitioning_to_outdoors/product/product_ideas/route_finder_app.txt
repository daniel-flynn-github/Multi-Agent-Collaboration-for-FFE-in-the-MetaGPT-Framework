Product: Route Finder App
Product Description: The Route Finder App is a mobile application designed to help indoor climbers transitioning to outdoor climbing find suitable climbing routes. The app uses GPS technology to identify the user's location and provides a comprehensive database of climbing routes in the area. It includes detailed information about each route, such as difficulty level, route type, and rock type. Users can filter routes based on their climbing preferences and skill level, making it easier to find routes that match their abilities. The app also allows users to read reviews and ratings from other climbers, providing valuable insights and recommendations. With the Route Finder App, climbers can confidently explore new outdoor climbing areas and discover routes that suit their skill level and interests.
